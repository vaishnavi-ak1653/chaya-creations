Our Story

Fashion Born from
Passion & Purpose

Chaya Creations was founded with a simple belief — clothing should feel as good as it looks. We blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to create pieces that are timeless yet utterly of-the-moment.

Every stitch tells a story of careful curation, sustainable choices, and a deep respect for the artisans who bring our vision to life. From the first sketch to the final garment, quality is our north star.

Chiguru

Little Sprouts, Big Style

Chiguru — meaning "new sprout" in Kannada — is our kidswear line crafted for the youngest members of the family. Soft, safe, and joyfully designed, every piece lets little ones move freely while looking wonderfully put-together.

Made with skin-friendly fabrics and thoughtful details, Chiguru brings the same design philosophy of Chaya Creations to sizes 0–12 years.

100% skin-safe, breathable fabrics
Sizes newborn to 12 years
Easy-wear fits for active kids
Playful yet refined designs
